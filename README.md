## Company Scenario

**OmniCare Health** is a mid-sized healthcare technology company (1,200 employees, $200M annual revenue) that delivers telemedicine platforms and patient data management solutions. Following an aggressive growth strategy вҖ” three acquisitions in 24 months вҖ” OmniCare has inherited disparate IT systems, decentralized decision-making, and no unified governance layer. IT investments are managed in silos across business units. There is no IT steering committee. Governance practices are ad-hoc at best. This assessment positions OmniCare for **SOX readiness** and **enterprise risk management maturity**.
